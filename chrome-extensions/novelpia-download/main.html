This plugin is used to download novels on web browsers. It supports downloading both complete novels and individual chapters.

Paid chapters (unpaid) are not supported.

Before using, please log in to your Novelpia account.

Supported URLs:
https://novelpia.jp/novel/*
https://novelpia.jp/viewer/*
https://novelpia.com/novel/*
https://novelpia.com/viewer/*

Navigate to the novel page you wish to download and click the button to begin.

After clicking the download button, please wait for a moment while the program gathers resource information. A "Chrome wants to save multiple files" pop-up may appear during the download process. Please click OK to proceed, and the download will begin shortly.
